Privacy Policy

Fred's Plumbing Service internal data integration. Last updated June 2026.

Scope

This software ("the Software") is a private, internal integration operated solely for Fred's Plumbing Service ("Fred's Plumbing"). It is not a public or multi-tenant product; no third party installs or uses it.

What data it accesses

The Software connects to Fred's Plumbing's own accounts in third-party services — including QuickBooks Online, FieldPulse, and ALN — to retrieve the company's own business records (such as customers, jobs, invoices, payments, expenses, and accounting structure). Access to QuickBooks Online is strictly read-only: the Software retrieves data and never creates, modifies, or deletes records.

How the data is used

Retrieved data is consolidated into a private database used solely to provide reporting, reconciliation, and analytics for Fred's Plumbing. The data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or any purpose unrelated to operating the Software for Fred's Plumbing.

Storage and security

Data is stored in a private database controlled by Fred's Plumbing, with access limited to authorized personnel. Credentials and access tokens are stored securely and are never published or shared.

Retention and revocation

Data is retained for as long as it is needed for the company's reporting and may be deleted on request. QuickBooks Online access can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the application from within QuickBooks Online, after which no further data is retrieved.
